What is it?

Birdwatching, or birding, is the observing of birds, either as a recreational activity or as a form of citizen science. A birdwatcher may observe by using their naked eye, by using a visual enhancement device such as binoculars or a telescope, by listening for bird sounds, watching public webcams, or by viewing smart bird feeder cameras. The hobby of studying wild birds in their natural environment.

Common birds

There are many common birds you can observe in your garden or nature. You can watch their behaviours when searching for food, building their nests, raising their young, interacting with their own or you and so much more.

Here are some common birds, you have for sure seen:

  • Robin
  • Blue Tit
  • Coal Tit
  • Great Tit
  • Canada Goose
  • Barn Owl

What are the stages of growth of birds?

  1. Egg
  2. Hatchling, or chick
  3. Nestling
  4. Fledgling
  5. Juvenile
  6. Sub-adult
  7. Adult

Each stage involves unique physiological and behavioral changes as the bird matures.
